2. Abstract / Introduction (
LViT-N is a complete AI-powered radiology pipeline, from a custom-built hybrid segmentation model to a clinical explainability application designed for real-world use. At its core, it accepts a medical image, sends it to our custom-trained LViT-N segmentation model, which then renders the predicted anatomical region in a segmented mask. Once segmented, our system generates a structured radiology report covering clinical findings and impressions. Each sentence in the report is individually hoverable, and on click, it explains the clinical finding in easy & non technical language so that a patient can understand.
